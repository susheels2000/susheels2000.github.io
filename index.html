Research interests

I am interested in Diophantine Geometry and its intersections with O-minimality


Past Research Experiences

In my master's thesis, I worked on the topic of complex cellular structures, a theory developed by Gal Binyamini and Dmitry Novikov, which provides a complex analytic approach to semialgebraic and subanalytic Geometry. Together with Gal, I provided a stronger,disjoint version of the Cellular Parametrization Theorem for Real Cells. Using this, we established a much more effective version of the Lion-Rolin Preparation Theorem for bounded semialgebraic functions. My Master's thesis is available here.

As Part of my Bachelor's Thesis, I worked on modular forms. I conducted an exposition on the congruence relations for coefficients of cusp forms, largely following the works of Swinnerton-Dyer. You can find a four page summary of the exposition here
